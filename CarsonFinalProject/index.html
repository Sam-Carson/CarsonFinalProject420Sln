Metorites

Metorites are asteriods or comets that have entered the Earth's atmosphere. The majority of Metorites burn up when entering the atmoshpere but a few manage to make it to the Earth's surface. Most Metorites are considered Micrometeorites and tend to be dust particles or very small in size.
